Motome Kimura is a 20-year-old-woman born and raised in the Yamaguchi prefecture. Her father is a clerk in the village. She is the second daughter of the Kimura family, with the eldest daughter being Naomi.

Motome attended a girl's high school. She was not necessarily considered ambitious, but rather enjoyed her routine life. Motome's marriage and travel abroad was arranged by her parents when they no longer were able to afford her schooling. 

She emigrated to California in 1917 and married Yasuda Kitano upon her arrival.
