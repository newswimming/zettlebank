
third-space simulacra is a semiotic space of figments and fabrications, a performative calling for hybridity and categorical breakings, a social construct of drive and sedimentations. The theatricality of third-space simulacra is not unlike an ideological mis-en-scene in which the theatricality of power is tied with the virtuality of imagination, and is a script that can be written together.
